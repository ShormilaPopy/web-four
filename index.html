Introduction of Bangladesh

The name of my nation is Bangladesh. Its location in eastern South Asia borders the Bay of Bengal in the south, India, Myanmar, and simply India to the east and west. Its total area, including waterways, is 147,570 square kilometers. It won its freedom following a bloody liberation conflict. Three million men and women have sacrificed their lives for independence. The newly formed country faced many hurdles and harsh times after gaining independence.
